Botanical Name: Aglaonema "Emerald Beauty"
Light Requirement: Works well in all light levels
Grow Pot Diameter: 14"
Fits in these Container Sizes: Large | X-Large
Container Shown in 1st photo: Barcelona (Large)

Specimen Details
Aglaonemas are very decorative plants and come in many leaf variegations. The "Emerald Beauty" is known for its darker foliage with a touch of lighter green. The foliage stays compact and full. They look great when kept with a low shine.

Light Requirement: Agloanemas can withstand a lower level of light, such as being far away from a window or in a room with artificial lights only.
Water: Keep on the dry side
Typical Average Size: 20/24" Wide | 26/28" Tall

Why We Love Them
- Air cleaning top performer!
- Very easy to maintain

Designer Advice
Perfect to be used on each side of a console table or buffet. Also great in an office next to a desk to hide all those unsightly wires.
