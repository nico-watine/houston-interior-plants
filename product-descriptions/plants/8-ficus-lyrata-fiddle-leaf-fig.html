A Best Seller!

With their unique and modern look, this 8' Ficus Lyrata is sure to bring a touch of originality to any interior.

Designer Advice

This bold looking indoor tree is a plant of choice for a bright large corner in any room of your home or office. If you have light and space, this is it! The fiddle leaf fig will fill up the space and become a focal point. With time it will shape into an interesting and unique tree, sending branches in all directions. Some pruning will easily keep it in shape.
