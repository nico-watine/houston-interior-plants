Specimen Details

Chinese Evergreen Aglaonemas are very decorative plants and come in many leaf variegations. This "Emerald Beauty" is known for its darker foliage with a touch of lighter green.

Light Requirement

Being a member of the Aglaonema family, this Chinese Evergreen can withstand a lower level of light. This makes it a strong choice for an area far from a window or in a room with artificial lights only.

Water

Water this Chinese Evergreen every 2 weeks to keep it on the dry side.

Variations

Chinese Evergreen | Large

Designer Advice

These are perfect for use on each side of a console table or buffet. The Chinese Evergreen is also especially great by an office desk to hide all of those unsightly wires!
