Specimen Details

Kentias Palms are beautiful, long living and easy to care for. This elegant specimin is often confused with the much cheaper, harder to care for Areca Palm, which is often sold at large retailers. Kentias are among the most striking interior plants and are definitely a premium choice.

Designer Advice

These Kentia are very elegant plants, and they will enhance any entry hall, living room or open space, such as a loft.
