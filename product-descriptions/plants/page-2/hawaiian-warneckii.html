Specimen Details

A member of the Dracaena family, our Warneckii canes are grown in Hawaii and are of unsurpassed quality. This is a rather under used plant which deserves more considerations when decorating with plants. We particularly love the white lines on the leaves, as they add a very beautiful contrast of colors.
