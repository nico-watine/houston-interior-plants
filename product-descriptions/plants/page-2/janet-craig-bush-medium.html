Botanical Name: Dracaena Deremensis "Janet Craig"
Light Requirement: Low
Grow Pot Diameter: 10"
Fits in these Container Sizes: Medium | Large | X-Large
Container Shown in 1st Photo: Barcelona - Espresso

Typical Average Size

24/28" Wide | 26/28" Tall

Specimen Details

"Janet Craigs," or JC bush as we call them, are an easy maintenance, long lasting indoor plant. The dark green foliage always makes for a nice contrast with its surroundings. It is a great gift plant as well. Always looks better when kept dust free and shiny!

Light

Janet Craigs can withstand a lower level of light, such as being far away from a window or in a room with artificial lights only.

Water

Check the Janet Craig every 2 weeks and water it when the top soil is dry to the touch.

Designer Advice

The Janet Craig bush is versatile and easy to place. It will work anywhere — next to a desk or a sofa, or even in front of a window.

Why We Love Them

- Air cleaning top performer

Variations

Janet Craig Carmen – (With a beautiful yellow stripe on the edges of its leaves, the Carmen makes for a nice alternative to the plain green)
