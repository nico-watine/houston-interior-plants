Botanical Name: Dracaena Deremensis "Lisa"
Light Requirement: Low
Grow Pot Diameter: 10"
Fits in these Container Sizes: Medium | Large | X-Large

Typical Average Size

18/22" Wide | 5/6ft Tall

Why We Love Them

- Air cleaning top performer
- Perfect for a corner at home or the office

Water

Give this Hawaiian Lisa Cane about 1 half quart of water once a week.

Designer Advice

The Hawaiian Lisa Cane is a very versatile plant. With its low light requirement and it's tall thin shape, it can be placed in any corner away from a window. These are a great alternative to the common Corn Plant. They are very low maintenance and have slicker, more updated look. While it does fit into Medium containers with its 10" pot, a Large container is recommended due to its height.

Variations
