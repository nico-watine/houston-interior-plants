Botanical Name: Dracaena Deremensis "Janet Craig Carmen"
Light Requirement: Low
Grow Pot Diameter: 14"
Fits in these Container Sizes: Large | X-Large
Container Shown in 1st Photo: Barcelona - Espresso

Typical Average Size

30/36" Wide | 32/46" Tall

Specimen Details

"Janet Craig Carmens," or JC Carmen bush, as we call them, are an easy maintenance, long lasting indoor plant. It features dark green foliage with beautiful yellow stripes down the edges.

Water

Check the Janet Craig Carmen every 2 weeks and water it when the top soil is dry to the touch.

Light

Janet Craig Carmens can withstand a lower level of light, such as being far away from a window or in a room with artificial lights only.

Variations

Janet Craig Bush | Medium
