Botanical Name: Sansevieria "Zeylanica"
Light Requirement: Can tolerate low and moderate levels of light, but bright filtered light is best
Grow Pot Diameter: 10"
Fits in these Container Sizes: Small | Medium | Large | X-Large
Container Shown in 1st Photo: Barcelona (Small)

Typical Average Size

10-12" Wide | 24-30" Tall

Water

The Mother-in-Law's Tongue is very easy to care for. Sansevierias can take of lot of neglect, but for best results, water it once a month and let it dry out between waterings.

Why We Love Them

This plant has been around for quite a while and is very popular right now, due to their simple ease of care and the health benefits the Mother-in-Law's Tongue provides. Read more about their health benefits at http://greenspaces.in/blog/ted09/
