Botanical Name: Chamaedorea Hybrid
Light Requirement: Medium - High
Grow Pot Diameter: 14"
Fits in these Container Sizes: Large | X-Large

Typical Average Size

18/22" Wide | 56/66" Tall

Specimen Details

Both beautiful and graceful, Bamboo palms are medium green palms. This Large size comes in a 14" grow pot and is usually about 5' tall.

Light Requirement

These bamboos require good filtered light, but cannot take direct sun.

Water

Keep the bamboo moist but not wet. Over watering will cause the tips to turn yellow, and leaves will start to fall.

Why We Love Them

-Top performing Air Cleaner!

Variations

Bamboo Palm | Medium

Designer Advice

This Bamboo palm retains its shape as it grows. This makes it a good choice for a corner in a bedroom or in an office next to a credenza.
